What is Fab4Frenzy?

Fab4Frenzy offers an exciting new format where 4 people form a team. There are only 6 teams that participate in a Fab4Frenzy tournament, 24 people. Every point counts in this tournament - the total number of points accumulated determines the order of finish. The number of wins will only come in play if there is a tie in the total number of points. Reporting takes place throughout the event. This is exciting because you can compare your accumulated points to the other teams throughout the competition and continuously know where you stand. This information will be updated promptly and you can view your progress by checking out the charts that will be posted.

Tournaments normally have the following characteristics:

Skill Levels

A tournament will cater to a certain level (anywhere from 2.5 to 4.0). Please ensure you register only if your level of play matches what we are looking for in a given tournament - do not participate if you should be playing at a higher level. We prefer that you wait until we host another Fab4Frenzy event for your level.

Age

There are no restrictions - all ages can participate.

Number of teams competing in an event

6 teams

Format
  • Teams consist of 4 players.
  • You play the first 5 games of the tournament with a partner from your team.
  • You play the next 5 games of the tournament with a different member of your team.
  • Each player participates in 10 total games.
  • Each team plays 20 total games.
  • There are 60 games played in the event overall.
Variants
  • Men: Each team has 4 men
  • Women: Each team has 4 women
  • Mixed: Each team has 2 men and 2 women
Categories

Tournaments can be held for a wide range of skill levels, genders and ages as follows:

Skill Levels:
From 2.5 to 4.5 (Learn about skill levels)

DUPR (for all tournaments except the 2.5 level)
