What is Fab4Frenzy?

Fab4Frenzy offers an exciting new format where 4 people (2 men and 2 women) form a team. There are only 6 teams that participate in a Fab4Frenzy tournament, 24 people. Every point counts in this tournament - the total number of points accumulated determines the order of finish. The number of wins will only come in play if there is a tie in the total number of points. Reporting takes place throughout the event. This is exciting because you can compare your accumulated points to the other teams throughout the competition and continuously know where you stand. This information will be updated promptly and you can view your progress by checking out the charts that will be posted just outside of the gym.

Tournaments normally have the following characteristics:

Gender

There are 2 men and 2 women in each team.

Levels

A tournament will cater to a certain level (anywhere from 2.5 to 4.0). Please ensure you register only if your level of play matches what we are looking for in a given tournament - do not participate if you should be playing at a higher level. We prefer that you wait until we hot another Fab4Frenzy event for you level.

Age

There are no restrictions - all ages can participate.

Number of teams competing in an event

6 teams (2 men and 2 women per team).

Format

Round robin

  • The 2 males on a team play 5 games against males on the other teams.
  • The 2 females on a team play 5 games against females on the other teams.
  • One male and a female on a team (mixed) compete against other mixed teams.
  • The other male and a female on a team (mixed) compete against other mixed teams - the 2 mixed pairings on a team will not play against each other.
  • Every person plays 10 games.
  • Every team plays 20 games.
  • There are 60 games in total for the event.

Categories

Tournaments can be held for a wide range of skill levels, genders and ages as follows:

Skill Levels:
From 2.5 to 4.5 (Learn about skill levels)

DUPR

  • To participate in this event, you must have a DUPR account. An account can be created free of charge at dupr.com.
  • Once you have a DUPR account, please join the South Winnipeg Pickleball League.
