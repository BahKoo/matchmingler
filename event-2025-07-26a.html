Fab4Frenzy - level 3.0

July 26th, 2025 - 8:30 am to 12:45 pm

Date July 26th, 2025
Time 8:30 am to 12:45 pm (Arrive at 8:00am for check-in)
Location Winnipeg (Exact Location will be given after you express interest)
Event Type Fab4Frenzy
Gender Men & Women
Level 3.0
Age Any
Number Of Teams 6
Format Round robin
  • The 2 males on a team play 5 games against males on the other teams.
  • The 2 females on a team play 5 games against females on the other teams.
  • One male and a female on a team (mixed) compete against other mixed teams.
  • The other male and a female on a team (mixed) compete against other mixed teams - the 2 mixed pairings on a team will not play against each other.
  • Every person plays 10 games.
  • Every team plays 20 games.
  • There are 60 games in total for the event.
Food/Drink Snacks and water will be provided
Cost $30 per person ($120 per team)
Status Available
