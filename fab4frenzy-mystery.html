What is Mystery Fab4Frenzy?

Mystery Fab4Frenzy is Fab4Frenzy with a twist!

Let's have a tournament where you don't pick partners. In this Fab4Frenzy event, your partners will be determined by drawing names out of a hat just prior to the tournament. That's why this is a mystery - you have no idea what your team will be until the start.

Skill Levels

A tournament will cater to a certain level (anywhere from 2.5 to 4.0). Please ensure you register only if your level of play matches what we are looking for in a given tournament - do not participate if you should be playing at a higher level. We prefer that you wait until we host another Fab4Frenzy event for your level.

Age

There are no restrictions - all ages can participate.

Number of teams competing in an event

6 teams

Format
  • Teams consist of 4 players.
  • You play the first 5 games of the tournament with a partner from your team.
  • You play the next 5 games of the tournament with a different member of your team.
  • Each player participates in 10 total games.
  • Each team plays 20 total games.
  • There are 60 games played in the event overall.
Skill Levels:
  • From 2.5 to 4.5 (Learn about skill levels)
