What is Match Mingler?

The Match Mingler website is currently being developed.

For now this site is dedicated to providing information about upcoming pickleball events in Manitoba. With time the plan is to expand and to include cities from across Canada and the United States.

Current Match Mingler Events

Match Mingler runs pickleball events for single people in Manitoba called Singles Mingle. We have 2 events currently being offered but all available spots have been taken. The events will take place in March and April of this year.
