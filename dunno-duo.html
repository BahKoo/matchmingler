What is Dunno Duo?

In this tournament, each participant plays 8 games - each game with a different randomly selected partner. You don't know (Dunno) ahead of time who you will be paired with (Duo).

Each team looks to make as many points as possible because this tournament is based on points, not on wins. Total points are accumulated for each participant.

When all participants have played 8 games, the top 8 individual finishers will play for medals in a single game to 15 points (win by 2) as follows:

Gold/Silver
Team 1 Player finishing 1st partners with player finishing 4th
Team 2 Player finishing 2nd partners with player finishing 3rd
Bronze
Team 1 Player finishing 5th partners with player finishing 8th
Team 2 Player finishing 6th partners with player finishing 7th
Skill Levels

A tournament will cater to a certain level (anywhere from 2.5 to 4.0). Please ensure you register only if your level of play matches what we are looking for in a given tournament - do not participate if you should be playing at a higher level. We prefer that you wait until we host another Dunno Duo event for your level.

Age

There are no restrictions - all ages can participate.
