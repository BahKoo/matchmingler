Awesome Twosome

Upcoming Events

What is this all about?

Add your age and your partner's age and if the sum falls into the combined age category specified for the event you can participate.

For example, if the event is for a 75-99 age category two 40-year-olds can team up. Also, a 67 year old parent can team up with a 31 year old child. How about a grandparent with a grandchild? No problem, as long as the combined ages do not exceed 99 (in a 75-99 age event). This makes it interesting and allows for younger players to participate in a tournament.

Format

  • 12 teams participate in an Awesome Twosome event.
  • There are 2 pools of 6 teams per pool.
  • Every team plays 5 games in the round robin and is guaranteed 1 playoff game.
  • The top 8 teams from the round robin will compete in the championship round while the 4 other teams will compete in consolations.

DUPR

  • To participate in this event, you must have a DUPR account. An account can be created free of charge at dupr.com.
  • Once you have a DUPR account, please join the South Winnipeg Pickleball League.
  • To learn more about DUPR, check out this article: Does a DUPR Rating Really Matter?
